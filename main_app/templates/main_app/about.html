About Django Unlibbed

Why?

Django was way too libbed. Our goal as Pythonistas Unchained--to unlib him. Our mission was to make a madlibs-style app based on the Django Unchained movie script.

Technology used

This web app is built with Python, Django, PSQL, AWS, and Heroku.
